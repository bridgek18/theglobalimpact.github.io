Our Mission

Change comes from informed and passionate people.

That's why The Global Impact works to interactively inform people on the various issues and problems with the environment in our world today. Instead of throwing a heavy load of unclear information about those issues, we provide a game that shows just how important leaving a carbon footprint is. In addition to this, The Global Impact also provides little but very helpful ways you could help keep our Earth happy and healthy for generations to come.
