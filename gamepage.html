Carbon Footprint Game

A person or group's carbon footprint is the amount of carbon dioxide and other carbon compounds emitted due to their consumption of fossil fuels. In short, it's the amount of CO2 you use in your day-to-day life through simple activities like turning on lights and buying groceries. The bigger your carbon footprint is, the more you're hurting your environment and the natural life around you
